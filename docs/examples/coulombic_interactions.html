Coulombic interactions between hoppers can be handled by ToFeT in the regenerate and fet mode. In the latter case, these interactions are activated by default. In the former case, you can activate the interactions by setting hopperInteractions 1 in your sim file. If you do so, you need to provide site energies in your xyz file rather than DE in your edge file (because the meaning of DE is vague when Coulombic interactions are included).
